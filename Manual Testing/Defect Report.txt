Defect Title: After transaction is done, wrong account balance is displayed

Description:
	(A) Expected Result: After the money transfer of Rs 3000 from Account Balance of Rs 15000,
	    it should display the remaining balance = Rs 12000
	(B) Actual Result: After the money transfer of Rs 3000 from Account Balance of Rs 15000,
	    it is showing remaining balance = Rs 9000
	(C) Steps to reproduce defect:
		1. Open Browser
		2. Open Bank website
		3. Login 
		4. Click on Money Transfer
		5. Enter beneficiary details
		6. Enter amount to be transferred
		7. Enter M-PIN
